Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe for chocolate chip cookies is simply amazing. This recipe does not take too long to make, only requires ingredients you would have at home, and is an amazing treat for anyone with a sweet tooth. These cookie are perfect for any situation. Mother's day, a birthday, or even a wedding. And if these don't get you laid, you really have no hope in life.

These cookies turn out perfect every time. My 3 year old cousin could make these with her eyes closed and one arm around her teddy bear. That might be a little big of an exaggeration. But the chocolate is perfectly paired with the light and delicous taste of the dough. These cookies crispy, but also chewy. Doughy yet perfectly baked. Buttery and also sweet. You tell me, what more could you want from chocolate chip cookies.
